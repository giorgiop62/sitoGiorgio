Ermes

I couldn't not tell you about Ermes, my inseparable friend for the past 7 years. Our bond goes beyond that of a man and his dog; it's something special and unique that has deeply shaped who I am. Ermes has been by my side through the happiest and the hardest times, with his silent but constant presence, always ready to make me smile with just a look. His free-spirited and playful nature has taught me to appreciate the little things in life, to live in the moment, and to find joy in the most ordinary days. Every step we take together reminds me how important it is to have someone who understands you without needing words. For this reason, I want to dedicate a special section of this website to him: anyone who knows me must also get to know Ermes, because he has changed the course of my life in ways I never imagined. Ermes is not just a dog; he is a part of me. It’s thanks to him that I’ve learned the strength of loyalty, the importance of companionship, and the beauty of unconditional love, given freely without expecting anything in return. He’s a friend, a companion in adventures, and a constant source of inspiration.
